my work experience

CREOTEC

2018

Back in 2018 during my SHS years, I was part of a 2-week work immersion at CREOTEC in Lapu-Lapu City. I worked in the Mass Production Department for iSense which is a product that when senses darkness, the buzzer sets an alarm. I was assigned to the mounting station using the tools and equipment provided that can be seen in one of the photos. Every day, we had to reach a target quota, which gets progressively higher, only within a limited number of working hours. Thus, we needed to work fast and efficient, only taking breaks during break time.

The work immersion is my first work experience. It was also my first time handling sensitive electric components and devices, and mounting them to a PCB. It was an experience full of lessons and learnings that I am grateful for, and I can't wait to use the skills and knowledge I have acquired in my career.
